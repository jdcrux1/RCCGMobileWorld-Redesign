The Story of RCCG Mobile World

What started as a small prayer group in Lagos, Nigeria in 1952 has become a global movement touching millions across 198 nations. Pastor Josiah Olufemi Akindayimo planted a seed of faith that continues to bear fruit today.

We exist to make heaven and take as many people with us. Our passion is to see every family, every neighborhood, and every nation reached with the love of God through vibrant local churches.

Here at RCCG Mobile World, we carry that same vision to our community — creating a space where authentic worship thrives, families are strengthened, and the next generation is equipped to impact their world.

Our Foundation

What We Believe

The core truths that guide our faith and practice.

The Holy Bible

God's inspired Word, our guide for life.

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice. It reveals God's heart and guides us in all areas of life.

Salvation by Grace

A free gift through faith in Jesus.

Salvation is a gift of God through faith in Jesus Christ — not by works, but by His amazing grace. Through His death and resurrection, we have eternal life.

The Holy Trinity

One God in three persons.

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. These three are one in essence but distinct in function.

Holy Spirit Baptism

Power for living and service.

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues and the operation of spiritual gifts for the edification of the Church.

Second Coming

Our hope for eternity.

We believe in the imminent return of Jesus Christ for His Church and the establishment of His eternal kingdom. Every knee will bow!

Divine Healing

God's provision for our bodies.

We believe in divine healing through prayer and the laying on of hands as part of the atonement of Christ. God still heals today!
