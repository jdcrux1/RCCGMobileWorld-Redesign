About RCCG

A community where faith ignites, hearts connect, and lives are transformed through the boundless love of God.

Our Story

A Legacy of Faith Since 1952

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) was founded in Lagos, Nigeria in 1952 by Pastor Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi. What began as a small prayer group has grown into one of the largest Christian denominations in the world, with parishes in over 198 nations.

Under the visionary leadership of Pastor E.A. Adeboye since 1981, RCCG has become a beacon of hope, planting churches, building communities, and raising leaders who impact every sphere of society.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make heaven, to take as many people with us, to have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations, and to accomplish this goal by planting churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town.

Our Vision

To build a church where the Spirit of God reigns supreme, where worship is vibrant, and every believer is equipped to fulfill their God-given purpose with boldness and impact across every sphere of life.

Core Beliefs

What We Believe

Our faith is rooted in the Word of God and the foundational truths of Christianity.

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice.

Salvation by Grace

Salvation is a gift of God through faith in Jesus Christ — not by works, but by His amazing grace.

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Holy Spirit Baptism

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues and the operation of spiritual gifts.

Second Coming

We believe in the imminent return of Jesus Christ for His Church and the establishment of His eternal kingdom.

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing through prayer and the laying on of hands as part of the atonement of Christ.
