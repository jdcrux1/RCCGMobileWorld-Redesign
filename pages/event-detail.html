About This Event

Youth Summit 2026 is a transformative event designed to empower young people aged 13-35 for excellence and leadership. Join us for a day of inspiring worship, powerful teaching, and networking with like-minded young people from across the nation.

What to expect:

  • Inspiring worship sessions led by talented youth worship teams
  • Leadership training workshops with industry experts
  • Networking opportunities with young leaders from across the country
  • Ministry exhibition showcasing youth-led initiatives
  • Special guest appearances and surprise performances

Don't miss this incredible opportunity to be empowered, inspired, and connected with the next generation of leaders!
