Who We Are

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) is a worldwide fellowship with a vision of propagating the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to the ends of the earth.

Our mission is to impact the world for Christ through the preaching of the Word and practical demonstration of the Holy Spirit's power.

Our Vision

To be a church where the presence of God is tangible, lives are transformed.

Our Mission

To present Jesus Christ to individuals and families, build them in the image of God.
